1) -en
    - most of the case
2) -s
    - after e, é, ie, el, em, en, er
3) -'s
    - a, i, o, u, y


-s comes after
--------------
-e      courgette       courgettes
-é      café            café's
-ie     vakantie        vakanties
-el     regel           regels
-em     modem           modems
-en     teken           tekens
-er     kamer           kamers

-s' comes after
---------------
-a      camera          camera's
-i      taxi            taxi's
-o      foto            foto's
-u      paraplu         paraplu's
-y      baby            baby's
